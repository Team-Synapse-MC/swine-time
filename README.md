So what exactly is Swine Time?

Swine Time started two years ago as a bedrock add-on made by @Worm with the simple concept of adding more pigs. Over years and countless redesigns, the mod finally has backing from Team Synapse to get it coded!

Meet the Dire Boar, a large and muscular pig that roams forests and mesas in large groups. Some say it’s the ancient ancestor of hoglins...

If you’re lucky enough to find a hearty potato when harvesting regular ones, you can try to tame the Dire Boar. If successful, the boar will let you ride it and you can even ram other mobs with it! If you instead decide to turn to a more violent path, it will drop a brand-new food source, Chunky Chops, that works like any other meat, but is much more saturating and filling.
